## 📊 Overview

An Excel-based data analysis project focused on identifying sales trends, top-performing products, and business insights using an interactive dashboard.

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this analysis was to identify:

* Peak sales hours and days
* Top-performing products
* Revenue trends over time
* Store-wise performance differences

## 📈 Key Insights

* Morning hours (8 AM – 10 AM) generate peak sales
* Coffee category contributes the highest revenue
* Hell’s Kitchen store outperforms other locations
* Top products include Ethiopian, Jamaican Coffee River, and Latte
* Sales show consistent growth from January to June

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Sales: 698,493
* Total Footfall: 149,117
* Avg Bill per Person: 4.68
* Avg Orders per Person: 1.44

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization (Charts & Dashboard)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Increase staffing during peak morning hours
* Promote low-performing products
* Apply strategies from top-performing locations
* Improve data quality (undefined order sizes)
